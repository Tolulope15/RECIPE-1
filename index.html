Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich and savory meat sauce. It's a family favorite that is both filling and delicious, perfect for weeknight dinners.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of spaghetti
  • 1.5 lb ground beef
  • 1 white onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 796 ml canned tomatoe diced
  • 1 can of tomatoes paste
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 chilli pepper

Spices

  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Rosemary
  • 2 Stock cubes

Instructions

BOIL PASTA

  1. Fill large pot with water, season it with salt till water is as salty as the ocean, stir and bring water to boil.
  2. Add pasta to water and stir to prevent the pasta from sticking together.
  3. Boil for 10- 15 minutes till it's semi-cooked.
  4. Drain pasta and set aside.

PREPARE BOLOGNESE

  1. In a pot, heat oil and fry onion and garlic till browned
  2. Add beef to pot and break apart with spoon as it cooks and browns. Season with salt, pepper and beef bullion
  3. Create a little space in pot and add in tomato paste. Allow it fry on the side till it begins to separate ( should take 5-7 minutes)
    then mix it with the meat
  4. Pour canned diced tomatoes into the pot and cook for 10-15 minutes
  5. Add in Worchesershire sauce and liquid smoke to your sauce( optional).
    Season again with salt, beef bullion, rosemary, oregano, bayleaves, 1 chilli pepper and sugar
  6. Add drained spaghetti into the pot of sauce with a little pasta water and stir well. Allow it cook for 5 minutes
  7. SERVE

Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine while the sauce simmers. You can also sprinkle grated parmesan cheese on top before serving.
